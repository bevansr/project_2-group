What do you know about Chicago Landmarks & Murals?


Landmarks





Chicago Landmark is a designation by the Mayor and the City Council of Chicago for historic sites in Chicago, Illinois, United States.

These sites are selected after meeting a combination of criteria, including historical, economic, architectural, artistic, cultural, and social values.


Murals





Spend some time walking around Chicago, and there's a good chance you'll see a mural or two. The city is dotted with public art, some well-known, much of it less so but often still a joy to happen on.

There are 356 registered murals in Chicago with a sea of themes including nature, religion, art, cartoons, sports, legacy etc.
